Our Story

Fashion with a conscience.

Founded in 2020, VŌGE exists at the intersection of considered design and sustainable craft. Every piece we create starts with a single question: will this still matter in ten years? We work with family-run factories across Portugal, Japan, and India — places where skill is passed down through generations and where quality is never compromised for speed.
